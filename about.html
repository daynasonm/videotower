NASA Video Tower

An interactive sculpture built from NASA's public Image and Video Library.

NASA Video Tower was inspired by the immersive visuals of Project Hail Mary and by the feeling of overstimulation that comes from being surrounded by more imagery than one can fully process. The vastness of outer space becomes a metaphor for content overload, creating a space that feels mesmerizing, enclosing, and difficult to escape.

Built with Three.js and MediaPipe FaceMesh.
